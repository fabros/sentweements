ChefChefGo is a simple recipe search engine.

Just type the terms you're interested in (e.g. "spaghetti carbonara") and press search.

Advanced operators let you refine your queries:

Union operator ||
Type two (or more) blocks of terms separated by || to get the union of the results of each block (e.g. "spaghetti carbonara || lasagna" to get recipes related to spaghetti carbonara or lasagna).

Negation operator -
You can exclude recipes that contain certain terms by prefixing them with the - operator (e.g. "spaghetti -artichokes" to get recipes for spaghetti that do not contain artichokes).

Diet filtering
You can refine your search appending the following keywords to your query to filter out results not compliant with your diet:
  • *vegetarian
  • *vegan
  • *lactose-int
